## ASIN TARGETING
### Product Targeting — Compete Directly with Similar Books

*Target specific competitor ASINs whose customers are likely to buy your book. Bid $0.35-$0.60.*

---

### Book #1 ASIN Targets (Bible Activity Books):

**How to Find Fresh ASINs:**
1. Search "bible activity book for kids" on Amazon
2. Note the ASIN (found in the URL: amazon.com/dp/**B0XXXXXXXX**)
3. Target books with:
   - 3.5-4.5 star ratings (beatable quality)
   - BSR under 100,000 in Books (proven demand)
   - Price between $7.99-$12.99 (similar price point)
   - Published in last 2 years (active market)

**Target Categories (use Category Targeting):**
- Children's Christian Activity Books
- Children's Bible Coloring Books
- Children's Religious Education
- Children's Word Search Books

**Target Types to Include:**
| Target Type | Description | Example |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| Direct Competitors | Same format (activity + coloring + puzzles) | Other Bible activity books ages 4-8 |
| Adjacent Format | Coloring-only Bible books | Bible coloring books (upsell with more activities) |
| Complementary | Bible storybooks | "They liked stories, they'll like activities too" |
| Gift Category | Children's baptism/communion gifts | Parents searching for religious gifts |

## CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE
### Recommended Setup for Launch

---

### Phase 1: Launch (Weeks 1-2)
**Goal:** Gather data, find winning keywords

| Campaign | Match Type | Daily Budget | Default Bid | Keywords |
|----------|-----------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Book1-Exact-Primary | Exact | $5/day | $0.55 | Campaign 1 (20 keywords) |
| Book1-Exact-Niche | Exact | $3/day | $0.40 | Campaign 2 (20 keywords) |
| Book1-Phrase | Phrase | $3/day | $0.35 | Campaign 3 (20 keywords) |
| Book1-Auto | Automatic | $5/day | $0.30 | (Amazon auto-targeting) |
| Book2-Exact-Primary | Exact | $5/day | $0.55 | Campaign 6 (20 keywords) |
| Book2-Exact-Niche | Exact | $3/day | $0.40 | Campaign 7 (20 keywords) |
| Book2-Phrase | Phrase | $3/day | $0.35 | Campaign 8 (20 keywords) |
| Book2-Auto | Automatic | $5/day | $0.30 | (Amazon auto-targeting) |

**Total Launch Budget: ~$32/day ($960/month)**

---

### Phase 2: Optimization (Weeks 3-4)
**Goal:** Scale winners, cut losers

1. **Review Search Term Reports** — find converting search terms from Auto and Phrase campaigns
2. **Graduate Winners** — move converting search terms to Exact match campaigns at higher bids
3. **Add Negatives** — add non-converting terms as negative keywords
4. **Increase Bids** — on keywords with ACoS under 40% (profitable)
5. **Decrease/Pause** — keywords with ACoS over 70% after 20+ clicks

---

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 2+)
**Goal:** Maximize profitable volume

1. **Scale winning campaigns** to $10-25/day budget
2. **Launch ASIN targeting campaigns** using competitor ASINs found during research
3. **Add seasonal campaigns** (see below)
4. **Test Sponsored Brands** headline ads (once you have 3+ books)
5. **Target ACoS:** 30-45% for growth phase, 20-30% for profit phase

---

### Key Metrics to Track:

| Metric | Target (Launch) | Target (Mature) |
|--------|----------------|-----------------|
| ACoS | Under 50% | Under 30% |
| CTR | Above 0.3% | Above 0.5% |
| Conversion Rate | Above 8% | Above 12% |
| CPC | Under $0.60 | Under $0.45 |
| Impressions/day | 1,000+ | 5,000+ |

## ADVANCED STRATEGIES

---

### 1. Keyword Bid Adjustments by Placement

| Placement | Bid Adjustment | Reason |
|-----------|---------------|--------|
| Top of Search | +25-50% | Highest conversion rate |
| Product Pages | +0-15% | Good for ASIN targeting |
| Rest of Search | Default bid | Discovery/testing |

---

### 2. Day-Parting Strategy
- **Increase bids:** Friday evening through Sunday (parents browse/buy on weekends)
- **Increase bids:** 8pm-11pm weeknights (after kids' bedtime, parents shop)
- **Decrease bids:** Monday-Thursday 9am-5pm (lower intent for children's books)

---

### 3. Cross-Promotion Between Books
Once both books are live, use each book's ads to promote the other:
- Add Book #2 ASIN as a target in Book #1 campaigns (readers of one want the other)
- Use Sponsored Brands to show both books together
- Include "Customers also bought" strategy by targeting your own ASINs

---

### 4. Review Velocity Strategy
- **Week 1-2:** Lower ACoS target (accept higher spend) to drive initial sales
- **Ask for reviews** via KDP's "Request a Review" button after delivery
- **Milestone:** 10+ reviews unlocks better ad performance (social proof)
- **Milestone:** 25+ reviews significantly improves conversion rate
- **Target:** 50+ reviews within 90 days for category authority

---

### 5. Monthly Keyword Refresh Checklist
- [ ] Check Search Term Report for new converting terms
- [ ] Add top converters to Exact match campaigns
- [ ] Add non-converters (20+ clicks, 0 sales) as negatives
- [ ] Check competitor launches (new ASINs to target)
- [ ] Adjust bids based on ACoS trends
- [ ] Activate/deactivate seasonal campaigns
- [ ] Test 3-5 new keywords per campaign per month

## QUICK-START CHECKLIST

**Before Running Ads:**
- [ ] Book is live on Amazon with optimized listing (title, description, keywords)
- [ ] A+ Content / Enhanced Brand Content set up (if Brand Registered)
- [ ] At least 1-2 initial reviews (friends/family legitimate purchases)
- [ ] Author Central profile complete with photo and bio
- [ ] Both paperback and Kindle versions linked on same listing
- [ ] Price set at $9.99 (competitive for category)

**Launch Day:**
- [ ] Start Auto campaign immediately (let Amazon find keywords)
- [ ] Start Exact match primary campaign same day
- [ ] Set budget and don't touch for 7 days (let data accumulate)
- [ ] Check ads daily but don't change bids for first week

**Week 2:**
- [ ] Review Search Term Report from Auto campaign
- [ ] Add top 5-10 search terms as Exact match keywords
- [ ] Add irrelevant search terms as negative keywords
- [ ] Launch Phrase match campaign
- [ ] Start ASIN targeting campaign

**Month 1 Review:**
- [ ] Calculate overall ACoS — target under 50% in month 1
- [ ] Identify top 10 performing keywords
- [ ] Pause keywords with 30+ clicks and 0 sales
- [ ] Scale budget on profitable campaigns (+20-30%)
- [ ] Plan Book #3 based on keyword research insights
