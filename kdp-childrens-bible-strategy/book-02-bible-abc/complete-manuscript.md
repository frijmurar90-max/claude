**Title:** My First Bible ABC Book: Learn Your Letters with God's Word
**Subtitle:** A Faith-Filled Alphabet Adventure for Toddlers and Preschoolers
**Author:** Selam Fesehaye
**Series:** Little Believers (Book 2)
**Target Age:** 2-5 years old
**Format:** 8.5" × 8.5" square, full color, 56 pages
**Price:** $9.99 paperback / $4.99 Kindle fixed-layout

---

## BOOK STRUCTURE

Each letter gets a 2-page spread:
- **Left Page:** Large uppercase & lowercase letter, Bible word, simple sentence, Bible verse
- **Right Page:** Full-page colorful illustration + small activity (trace letter, find object, count items)

**Total:** 26 spreads (52 pages) + 2 title/intro pages + 2 closing pages = 56 pages

**Dear Parents & Caregivers,**

This book introduces your little one to the alphabet through the beautiful stories and words of the Bible. Each letter features:
- A Bible word your child will love
- A simple, age-appropriate sentence
- A short Bible verse (ICB/NLT)
- A colorful illustration to explore together
- A small activity for little hands

Read it together, point to the pictures, and watch your child fall in love with both letters AND God's Word!

With love and blessings,
