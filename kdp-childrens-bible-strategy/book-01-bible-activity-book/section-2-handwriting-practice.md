# SECTION 2: BIBLE VERSE HANDWRITING PRACTICE

**Book:** Bible Activity Book for Kids Ages 4-8
**Author:** Selam Fesehaye
**Pages:** 41–65 (25 pages total)

---

## DESIGNER/ILLUSTRATOR NOTES:

- **Font for tracing letters:** Use a large dotted/dashed font (minimum 72pt for uppercase letters, 60pt for lowercase)
- **Font for tracing words:** Dotted/dashed font at 36-48pt
- **Font for verse text (printed model):** Clear, rounded sans-serif (like Sassoon Primary or similar child-friendly font) at 24-28pt
- **Tracing lines:** Gray dotted lines with 0.75-inch line height for younger kids
- **Blank writing lines:** Solid baseline with dotted midline, 0.5-inch line height
- **Margins:** Minimum 0.75 inches all sides
- **Page size:** 8.5 x 11 inches
- **Color palette:** Soft pastels — light blue, gentle yellow, soft pink, mint green
- **Illustrations:** Simple, clean line-art style; small corner decorations (max 1.5 x 1.5 inches)

## Production Specifications:

### Paper & Printing:
- **Paper weight:** 60-70 lb text weight (thick enough to prevent bleed-through)
- **Print:** Black and white interior with spot color accents (soft blue or gold)
- **Binding:** Perfect bound (standard KDP)

### Typography:
- **Tracing font:** Dotted/dashed style — recommend "Trace" font family or similar
- **Display font (verse headers):** Rounded sans-serif — recommend "Sassoon Primary" or "Comic Neue"
- **Body text (instructions):** Clean sans-serif at 14-16pt

### Spacing Requirements:
- **Letter tracing (Sub-Section A):** 72pt uppercase, 60pt lowercase, minimum 1.25-inch vertical space per letter block
- **Word tracing (Sub-Section A):** 36-48pt dotted font
- **Verse tracing (Sub-Sections B & C):** 28-32pt dotted font on 0.75-inch ruled lines
- **Independent writing lines:** 0.5-inch with solid baseline and dotted midline
- **Drawing boxes (Sub-Section C):** Minimum 3 inches tall with scalloped or dashed border

### Color Coding Suggestion:
- Sub-Section A headers: Soft blue
- Sub-Section B headers: Warm gold/yellow
- Sub-Section C headers: Gentle green
- All tracing content: Gray dotted (50% opacity black)
- Decorative elements: Match section color

### Accessibility Notes:
- All illustrations should be simple line art appropriate for ages 4-8
- Tracing dots should be clearly visible but light enough to trace over
- Sufficient contrast between printed text and tracing guides
- Lines should be wide enough for children still developing fine motor skills
