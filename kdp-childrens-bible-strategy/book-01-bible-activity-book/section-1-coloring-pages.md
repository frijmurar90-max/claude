## Spread 1: God Created the World

### LEFT PAGE (Page 1)

---

**Genesis 1:1**

# "In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth."

---

**What does this mean?**

Before anything else existed, God made everything! He made the sky, the land, the oceans, the animals, and even you! Everything beautiful in our world was made by God.

---

🎨 **Color this page!**

---

**Did You Know?** God made the whole world in just six days and then rested on the seventh day. That's why we have a special day to rest and remember God each week!

---

### RIGHT PAGE (Page 2) — Full-Page Coloring Illustration

**ILLUSTRATION DESCRIPTION:**

**Main Scene/Characters:**
- A joyful, bright scene showing the creation of the world. A large, round Earth sits at the center of the page, with simple outlines of continents visible.

**Key Objects to Include:**
- Sun with bold rays on one side, crescent moon and stars on the other
- Fluffy clouds above the Earth
- Simple flowers, trees, and mountains on the Earth's surface
- A few friendly animals (elephant, bird, fish jumping from water) around the edges
- Light beams radiating from the top of the page (representing God's creative power)

**Suggested Background Elements:**
- Swirling cosmic patterns (simple spirals and dots) filling the space around Earth
- Stars scattered throughout the background

**Decorative Border Elements:**
- Border of alternating suns, moons, stars, and flowers in thick outlines

**Art Notes:** Thick bold outlines throughout. Simple shapes with large coloring areas. Earth should be big and central. All elements should be easily distinguishable with clear separation between coloring areas. Suitable for ages 4-8.
