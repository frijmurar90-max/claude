### PAGE 86 — MAZE 1: "Help Noah's Animals Find the Ark!"

**Difficulty Level:** Easy

**Starting Point:** A group of paired animals (two lions, two birds, two rabbits) standing together on the left side of the page.

**Ending Point:** Noah's Ark — a large wooden boat with a ramp leading up to the door, positioned on the right side of the page.

**Maze Path Shape/Theme:** The maze paths are shaped like gentle rolling hills and fields. The corridors are wide (appropriate for ages 4–5) with only 2–3 dead ends. The overall maze footprint is rectangular, approximately 6" × 5".

**Decorative Elements:**
- Raindrops falling lightly around the border of the maze
- Small flowers and bushes along the outer edges
- A gray sky with clouds above the maze
- Noah waving from the Ark door at the finish point


**Instruction Text:** "Help the animals find their way to Noah's Ark before the rain comes! Trace a path from the animals to the Ark."

**Bible Verse:** *"So Noah did everything exactly as God had commanded him."* — Genesis 6:22 (NLT)

**Layout Notes:**
- Maze occupies the center 70% of the page
- Title in large, bold, playful font at the top
- Bible verse in a cloud-shaped text box at the bottom
- Animals at the start are uncolored (child can color them after solving)

### PAGE 94 — DOT-TO-DOT 1: "Connect the Dots to Reveal Noah's Ark!"

**Number of Dots:** 25 (numbered 1–25)

**What the Completed Picture Reveals:** The outline of Noah's Ark — a large wooden boat with a curved hull, a cabin structure on top, and a small window. When connected, the dots trace the full silhouette of the Ark.

**Dot Placement Description:**
- Dots 1–8: Trace the bottom curve of the hull (left to right)
- Dots 9–12: Trace up the right side of the boat
- Dots 13–18: Trace the cabin/house structure on the deck
- Dots 19–22: Trace down the left side of the cabin back to the deck
- Dots 23–25: Complete the left side of the hull back to dot 1

**Additional Coloring Instruction:** "After you connect all the dots, color the Ark brown! Draw some animals peeking out of the window. Can you add raindrops falling from the sky?"

**Decorative Elements:**
- Wavy water lines pre-drawn beneath where the Ark will appear
- Small pre-drawn raindrops in the sky
- Two birds (doves) pre-drawn flying near the top of the page
- A small rainbow peeking through clouds in the upper corner (pre-drawn, ready to color)

**Bible Verse:** *"Noah did everything just as God commanded him."* — Genesis 6:22 (NIV)

**Layout Notes:**
- Dots are large (8pt) and clearly numbered in sequence
- Numbers are placed just outside each dot for easy reading
- Generous spacing between dots for small hands
- The unconnected dots should still give a subtle hint of the shape
- Title in large, playful font at top
- Bible verse at bottom in a water-wave shaped box

### PAGE 101 — SPOT THE DIFFERENCE 1: "Noah's Ark"

**Scene Description:** Two nearly identical images side by side showing Noah's Ark scene. The Ark is centered on calm water. A rainbow arches overhead. Animals are boarding the ramp in pairs — two giraffes, two elephants, two birds flying above. Noah stands at the top of the ramp with arms outstretched. Clouds are in the sky, and a dove flies near the rainbow.

**Image Layout:** Two rectangular images placed side by side (each approximately 3.5" × 4"), labeled "Picture A" and "Picture B."

**Differences Between the Two Images (6 total):**
1. In Picture B, there is an extra parrot sitting on top of the Ark (not in Picture A)
2. In Picture B, the rainbow is missing one color stripe (only 5 stripes instead of 6)
3. In Picture B, the dove is carrying an olive branch (in Picture A, its beak is empty)
4. In Picture B, one of the elephants is facing the opposite direction
5. In Picture B, there is an extra cloud in the upper-right corner
6. In Picture B, Noah's robe is a different color/pattern (stripes instead of solid)

**Instruction Text:** "Look carefully at both pictures of Noah's Ark. Can you spot 6 differences between Picture A and Picture B? Circle each difference you find!"

**Answer Box:** A small upside-down answer key at the very bottom of the page in tiny text.

**Bible Verse:** *"Two of every kind of bird, animal, and creature will come to you."* — Genesis 6:20 (NIV)

**Layout Notes:**
- Title in large, fun font at the top
- "Can you find 6 differences?" in a banner below the title
- Both pictures are identical in style — friendly, cartoon, colorful
- Small numbered circles (1–6) at the bottom for children to track their finds
- Bible verse in a boat-shaped text box at the bottom
