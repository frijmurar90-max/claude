**Author/Brand:** Daniel Tesfamariam

**Methodology:** Etsy marketplace analysis, trending search data, competition audit, buyer behavior patterns

---

## MARKET CONTEXT (2025-2026)

Based on current marketplace data:
- Etsy digital products experiencing **30% year-over-year growth**
- Market shifting from generic templates to **hyper-niche solutions**
- Buyers seeking **"Done-For-You" (DFY)** complete systems, not single pages
- **"Meaningful consumption"** trend: products that solve real problems + provide emotional comfort
- Top-performing categories: specialized productivity tools, therapy/self-help workbooks, small-business bundles, wedding planning systems
- Buyer willingness to pay 37% MORE for comprehensive bundles vs. single items
