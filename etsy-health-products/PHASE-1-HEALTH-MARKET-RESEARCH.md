## KEY MARKET INSIGHTS

1. **Comprehensiveness wins:** Buyers pay 3-5x more for "complete systems" vs. simple trackers
2. **Education + Tracking combo:** Products that TEACH + TRACK outperform either alone
3. **Condition-specific beats generic:** "Anti-inflammatory diet for joint pain" > "healthy eating guide"
4. **Action-oriented language:** "Recovery," "Management," "System" convert better than "Journal" or "Log"
5. **Bundle strategy critical:** Single products priced $9-14; bundles at $19-29 drive higher AOV
6. **Medical disclaimer required:** Include disclaimer on all health products (not medical advice)
